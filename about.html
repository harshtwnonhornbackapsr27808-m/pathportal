About PortalPath

We are an independent informational resource dedicated to helping people understand how to use online patient portals like MyChart — clearly, simply, and without confusion.

Our Mission

Making Patient Portals Easy to Understand

Millions of people use online patient portals like MyChart every day — yet many users find the experience confusing, especially when accessing for the first time or after being locked out.

PortalPath was created to fill that gap. We provide clear, step-by-step informational content that explains how portals like MyChart work, how they are typically accessed, and how common issues are resolved — all in plain, everyday language.

We are not affiliated with any portal provider, healthcare system, or technology company. We are simply an independent educational resource that helps people find the guidance they need.

Clear & Focused

No Personal Data Collected

PortalPath has no connection to MyChart, Epic Systems, or any healthcare provider or portal operator.

What We Do

The Information We Provide

Our content covers the most common topics people search for when they need help understanding how MyChart and other patient portals function.

🔑

Portal Access Explanations

We explain how the MyChart access screen works, what fields are typically required, and how users are authenticated by the system.

🔄

Account Recovery Guidance

We outline the general process most portals — including MyChart — use to help users recover access when they have forgotten their credentials.

🗺️

Navigation Walkthroughs

We describe how MyChart dashboards are typically structured, where key sections are located, and how to move between different areas of the portal.

📋

Feature Overviews

We summarize what features users typically find inside MyChart — including appointment scheduling, record viewing, and secure messaging options.

📱

Mobile Access Information

We explain how MyChart and similar portals can generally be accessed from mobile browsers and dedicated apps on smartphones and tablets.

📞

Official Support Direction

When direct help is needed, we guide users toward the appropriate official support channels for their specific portal or healthcare organization.
